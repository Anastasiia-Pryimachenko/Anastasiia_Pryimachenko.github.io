'Akademik Vernadsky'is the Ukrainian Antarctic station that is located on Galindez Island. Firstly, it was belong to the United Kingdom that hold the name Faraday station. In a while after the collapse of the USSR, on November 21, 1994 'Vidrodzhennia' allocated 12.000 dollars for the project 'Ukraine goes back to Antarctica'. After several expeditions the British flag was flown at half-mast and the Ukrainian state flag was flown at the station in February 1996. The Ukrainian station was named after Volodymyr Vernadsky, a distinguished scientist who founded the biosphere and noosphere doctrines and served as the first President of Ukraine's Academy of Sciences (1918). Reporting from February 2023, the 'Akademik Vernadsky' station shared a video-data about 1300 newborn penguins. While the amount of adult penguins there is already up to 3000 ones.
